The six stops

City by city, in ride order

港
STOP 01 · OCT 9–11

Hong Kong

香港
Courtyard Sha Tin · King River View
In: CX801 lands 9:00 PM Fri
Out: G902 from West Kowloon, Sun — at the station by 9:30 AM

Don't miss

  • Ten Thousand Buddhas Monastery — 430 statue-lined steps, next door to the hotel. Saturday 9 AM at opening. The one new thing.
  • Central on foot — Tai Kwun, Man Mo Temple, the mid-levels escalator — then Star Ferry upper deck at dusk and the TST skyline promenade.
  • Temple Street for dinner in the night market.

Eat

  • Yat Lok roast goose or Tim Ho Wan for Saturday lunch.
  • Australia Dairy Co. scrambled-egg toast in Jordan, Sunday on the way to the train. Cash, 30 minutes.

The tailor, if doing it

  • Sam's Tailor — WhatsApp two weeks ahead. 9:40 AM measure, 5:45 PM fitting after the Star Ferry. Tell them up front to RE-MEASURE — you're ~55 lbs lighter than their record. Everything must close Saturday.
沪
STOP 02 · OCT 11–13

Shanghai

上海
Westin Bund Center · King City View
In: G902 arrives ~8 PM Sun
Out: morning train to Xi'an, Tue

Don't miss

  • The Bund at night, straight off the train — 10-minute walk from the Westin; drop bags and go. The lights die at 10 PM.
  • Shanghai Tower at sunset, ~5 PM Monday. Booked ahead.
  • Yuyuan Bazaar + the zig-zag bridge at 8:15 AM, before the buses. The inner garden is closed Mondays — the bazaar is 80% of the postcard.

Eat

  • Xiaolongbao crawl on Huanghe Rd: Jia Jia Tang Bao + Yang's Fried Dumplings, side by side. It's hairy-crab season — order anything with crab roe.

Skip

  • Yu Garden interior (closed Mondays) · Tianzifang · the river cruise if the Bund already delivered.
秦
STOP 03 · OCT 13–16 · THE LONG STOP

Xi'an

西安
Hilton Xi'an · King Executive
Two full days — the anchor of the trip

Don't miss

  • Terracotta Warriors at the 8:30 opening, Wednesday — Pit 1 first, then the bronze chariots. Hire a licensed guide inside (~¥250); without context it's a hangar of statues.
  • City Wall bike ride, Thursday 8 AM — the full 14-km loop on top of a Ming wall. Best 90 minutes in the city.
  • Muslim Quarter — recon Tuesday night, full graze Thursday. Best after dark.

Eat

  • Roujiamo · biangbiang noodles · yangrou paomo at Laomijia Dayu. Eat on 西羊市 / 大皮院 / 洒金桥 — walk past the main drag.

Evenings

  • Song of Everlasting Sorrow outdoor spectacle at Huaqing Palace, Wednesday night. Confirm season.
  • Datang Everbright City lights + the free 8:30 PM fountain show, Thursday.

Skip

  • Bell / Drum Tower climbs · the Tang Dynasty dinner show · every jade-shop detour a driver proposes.
蜀
STOP 04 · OCT 16–18

Chengdu

成都
Grand Hyatt · King
One Saturday · dawn departure Sunday

Don't miss

  • Panda Base at the 7:30 opening — cubs first. Gone by 11:30; after 10:30 it's nap time. Booked ahead.
  • Heming Teahouse, People's Park, Saturday afternoon — bottomless jasmine, the ear cleaning (do it), the marriage market. Best sit-and-watch-China hour of the trip.
  • Shufeng Yayun Sichuan opera, 8 PM — face-changing up close. Booked ahead.

Eat

  • Friday: hotpot near the hotel — split pot 鸳鸯锅, order 微辣 "mild." Chengdu mild is American hot. Sesame-oil dip mandatory.
  • Saturday lunch: mapo tofu at Chen Mapo Tofu — the restaurant that invented it in 1862.

Skip

  • Kuanzhai Alleys (rebuilt mall-China) · the Leshan day trip · Jinli only as a Friday-dusk lantern stroll. Sunday = zero sightseeing, early train.
桂
STOP 05 · OCT 18–20

Guilin

桂林
Shangri-La · Horizon River View King
In: the triple-train Sunday
One Monday in the karst

The day — Xingping + Yangshuo, DIY

  • 8 AM train (25 min) → bamboo raft past the ¥20-bill view. Bring a ¥20 note for the photo. Beats the 4.5-hour cruise: a third the cost, the same climax scenery, at water level.
  • Climb Laozhai Hill for the panorama nobody does → beer-fish 啤酒鱼 lunch in the old town.
  • E-bike the Yulong River loop in Yangshuo → ~8:30 PM train back. Both trains pre-booked.

Eat

  • Guilin rice noodles, ¥10 — the local breakfast religion. Monday pre-train and again Tuesday.

Skip

  • Longji rice terraces (5–6 hrs transit, rice likely cut) · Elephant Trunk Hill entry · Seven Star Park. Sunday night: nothing — you just rode three trains.
穗
STOP 06 · OCT 20–22 · THE EATING CITY

Guangzhou

广州
Crowne Plaza City Centre · King Junior Suite
Out Thu: Didi to CAN Terminal 3 at 7:10 AM — NOT Metro Line 3

Don't miss

  • Dim sum brunch at Guangzhou Restaurant (est. 1935), Wednesday 8:15 AM — har gow, char siu bao, cheung fun. Solo gets seated fast. The anchor of the day.
  • Canton Tower at sunset, Tuesday ~5:45 PM up. The whole tower lights up at dark.
  • Pearl River night cruise, Wednesday ~7:30 PM. Book 1–2 days out.

Eat — Wednesday is five stops on foot, by design

  • Wu Cai Ji wonton noodles in its hidden Liwan alley — ¥20, Michelin Bib.
  • Nanxin double-skin milk · roast goose at Zhu Zai Ji · a second light dim sum round at Dian Dou De.

If there's room

  • Chen Clan Ancestral Hall (¥10, best hour of culture in town) · Shamian Island stroll. Cut Shamian before you cut any food.

What can actually sink this

The five real tripwires

Meds

Stimulant ADHD meds are effectively banned in mainland China. Prescriber call Aug 10. Don't board with this unresolved.

Visa

Apply July 13 via COVA — nothing else matters if this slips. DC embassy, courier service, 3–4 weeks door-to-door. Backstops calendared Aug 3 and Aug 31.

Real-name everything

Trains, Warriors, pandas are all tied to the passport number and booked weeks ahead. The calendar reminders are the system — don't freelance.

The Oct 18 triple-train day

Chengdu → Chongqing → Guiyang → Guilin all hinges on the first train. Confirm all three legs ticketed before leaving Xi'an.

The tea-ceremony scam

Anyone who approaches you in English near a tourist site and suggests going anywhere: smile, no, keep walking. Aimed exactly at solo American men.
